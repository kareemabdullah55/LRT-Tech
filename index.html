Our Promoter

The promoter is responsible for representing the brand within points of sale by showcasing and promoting products in a professional manner that reflects the company’s identity. They explain the key technical features and benefits to customers in order to boost sales and increase product awareness. The promoter is also committed to collecting feedback from customers and monitoring competitors in the market, then reporting these insights regularly to the direct manager to support continuous improvement and development. In addition, the promoter participates in regular training programs aimed at enhancing their skills in customer service, product knowledge, and presentation techniques, ensuring an exceptional customer experience and achieving the company’s marketing objectives.
